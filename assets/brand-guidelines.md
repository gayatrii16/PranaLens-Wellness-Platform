# PranaLens Wellness Brand Snapshot

## Brand Positioning
PranaLens Wellness is a premium preventive wellness company that blends AI-assisted interpretation with Ayurveda-informed wellbeing frameworks, acupressure-inspired diagnostics, and human-centered service delivery.

## Visual System
- Primary background: `#04141F`
- Surface tone: `rgba(10, 34, 49, 0.58)`
- Primary accent: `#5EEAD4`
- Secondary accent: `#7DD3FC`
- Support accent: `#7DD3A7`
- Typography: `Outfit` for headlines, `Manrope` for body text
